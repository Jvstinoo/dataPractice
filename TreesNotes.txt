Types of Trees:

- Balanced Tree
	All of its paths have the same length of differ by one.

- Complete Tree
	All of the levels should be full, the last one does not need to.

- Full Tree
	Every node has 0 or 2 children.

Traversal of trees:

-In-order
	L, Rt, R
-Pre-order
	Rt, L, R
-Post-order
	L, R, Rt
